Certify Safety Tools

TheRain Certify Safety Tools

Every ride on TheRain is protected by layered safety tools — verified drivers, CCTV monitoring, live trip sharing, and emergency SOS.

Live Trip Sharing
Share your ride in real time with trusted contacts so family and friends can follow your journey from pickup to drop-off.
AI Fraud Detection
Real-time monitoring detects GPS spoofing, fake screenshots, duplicate accounts, and suspicious payment activities.
Trust Score System
Drivers and riders each have a trust score built from ride history, ratings, and safety behavior — visible to both parties.
CCTV Monitoring
Every vehicle has interior and exterior cameras. Footage is securely stored on TheRain's private servers.
Emergency SOS
One-press SOS for drivers and riders, with a silent mode. Alerts go directly to our emergency response team.
AI Driver Verification
Every driver goes through identity verification, facial recognition, license scanning, and background checks before joining.
