Step by Step

Your Complete Ride Journey

From downloading the app to reaching your destination — here's exactly how TheRain works for you.

Download TheRain App
Get the TheRain app for free on Google Play Store or Apple App Store. Available for Android and iOS. The app is lightweight, fast, and works even on slow internet connections — optimized for Cameroon's network conditions.
Also available as Guest — no account needed!
Create Your Account
Sign up with your phone number and verify with a 6-digit OTP code sent via SMS. Your account is secured with JWT authentication and device fingerprinting. You can also book as a guest without creating an account.
Your data is encrypted and protected
Set Your Pickup and Destination
Enter your pickup location (or use GPS auto-detect) and your destination. Choose from Classique, VIP, Delivery, or School Transport. See the estimated fare and travel time before confirming your booking.
Schedule rides up to 7 days in advance
Get Matched with a Verified Driver
Our AI dispatch system instantly matches you with the nearest available verified driver. All drivers have passed identity verification, face matching, license scanning, and background checks. Track your driver live on the map.
Live GPS tracking throughout your ride
Pay Your Way
Pay with MTN Mobile Money, Orange Money, Visa card, bank transfer, or cash. All payments are secure and receipts are sent via SMS and email. Rate your driver after the ride to help maintain quality standards.
Rate your driver to improve service quality
