Quick Answers

Common Questions

Find quick answers before reaching out.

How quickly will you respond to my message?
We aim to respond to all messages within 2 hours during business hours. For urgent matters, call us directly at +237 676 011 861 or +237 674 321 486 — we are available 24/7.
Can I contact TheRain on WhatsApp?
Yes. You can reach us on WhatsApp at +237 676 011 861. Send us a message any time and our support team will get back to you as soon as possible.
Does TheRain offer services for schools and businesses?
Yes. TheRain offers a dedicated School Transport service with verified drivers, PIN-based pickup verification, and live parent tracking. For businesses, we have Fleet Management solutions tailored to corporate mobility needs. Contact us at info@therain.tech to discuss your requirements.
How do I apply to become a TheRain driver?
Download the TheRain app, tap 'Become a Driver', and complete the registration form. You will need a valid driver's license, vehicle registration documents, and a national identity card. Our team reviews applications within 2–3 business days.
